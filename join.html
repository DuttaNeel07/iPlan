Join iPLAN

Membership in iPLAN is currently free for ACM members. By joining, you will be added to the iPLAN mailing list and receive announcements about conferences, workshops, schools, hackathons, and other activities associated with the chapter.

Who should join?

  • Researchers in programming languages, compilers, and related formal methods.
  • Graduate and undergraduate students interested in PL and systems.
  • Compiler engineers, language designers, and software practitioners.
  • Faculty members teaching programming languages or compilers courses.
  • Anyone who cares about advancing the PL and compilers ecosystem in India.

If you are not yet an ACM member, please first become a member via the ACM India site, and then complete the iPLAN membership form below.

Membership Benefits

  • iPLAN mailing list announcements
  • Early access to event registrations
  • Network with India's PL & compilers community
  • Discounted rates at ACM SIGPLAN events
